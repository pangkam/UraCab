About

UraCab is an unregistered Private Limited Company established in 2023 by a group of like-minded educated youths from the State of Nagaland with a view to bring services like online taxi booking system to the State, similar to what companies like OlaCabs and Uber provide in other metrocities but on a smaller scale keeping in mind the State’s demography and topography.

Services

What we offer

UraCab is a mobile application offering one-click taxi booking system within the state of Nagaland. Through the use of UraCab app, the user will have the convenience of booking taxi rides by simply adding their pick-up and drop locations; the app will also allow live tracking and SOS button thereby making it safe and reliable; fares will be calculated according to the distance between the pick-up and drop location; moreover, the app will be userfriendly and designed to suit people of all ages with access to a smartphone.

Mission & Vision Statements

Some of the issues UraCab aims on tackling include safety of passengers and driver party, easing traffic congestion, minimizing pollution, providing fair taxi fares, making taxi services more widely available within the state, etc. The Company’s main objective is to make travel for residents as well as tourists easy, accessible, safe and reliable. In order to achieve this, the UraCab app and website are presently under works and we aim to have it developed by the end of March, 2023, after which there will be testing and prototype before it is open to the public for use.
